Andrea Yew

Founder and author of Choke Clinch Crank Combat

Andrea Yew is the author and founder of this passion project She graduated from the University of Edinburgh and is currently based in Singapore. Her creative work is featured in various anthologies such as Twin Cities, Asingbol, Anima Methodi and SingPoWriMo 2018. She launched her debut collection, In These Curved Spaces in 2019 and is also the co-editor of Crazy Little Pyromaniacs She is also a contributor to the anthology Making Kin. Her hobbies include strangling people on the mats when she is not buried under a pile of marking.
